Is Your State Warming?

Climate change doesn't mean it's always hot outside, or that the world will warm uniformly. The average temperature of the United States was below 20th century historical averages as recently as February 2015. You can use the following visualization to check the temperature anomaly levels for the contiguous United States or the state you live in. Data ranges from 1895 to 2014. Years marked in red are the ten most recent years. Hover over a line to see data for that year. (Note: does not include Alaska or Hawaii.)
