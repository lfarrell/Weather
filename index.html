Is Your State Warming?

Climate change doesn't mean it's always hot outside, or that the world will warm uniformly. Notice that though the world hasn't had a below average monthly temperature since 1984. The average temperature of the United States was below 20th century historical averages as recently as February 2015. Also note, there is much less variability in world temperatures compared to US temperatures.
